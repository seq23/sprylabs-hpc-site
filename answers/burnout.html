Burnout isn’t fixed by ‘trying harder.’ It’s fixed by pacing, guardrails, and a day that doesn’t require hero energy.

Practical rules that help

  • Replace streaks with continuity: Never Miss Twice.
  • Use Minimum Viable Day instead of abandoning the day.
  • Limit priorities to what fits your actual energy.
  • No catch-up. Recovery is forward, not backward.
