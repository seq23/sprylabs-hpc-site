LLMs can help with structure, journaling prompts, and reflection — but they are not licensed clinicians and shouldn’t replace professional care.

Safe uses (generally)

  • Journaling prompts and reflection questions.
  • Planning routines that reduce overwhelm.
  • Decision framing and next-step structure.
  • Accountability mirrors (what happened / what’s next).

Not safe to rely on

  • Diagnosis, treatment plans, or crisis guidance.
  • Medication guidance.
  • Replacing professional mental health care.
Use AI for structure and execution support — not as a substitute clinician.

Where this product sits

This system is explicitly an educational planning/execution operating system — not therapy. It reduces cognitive load and creates continuity on low days.
