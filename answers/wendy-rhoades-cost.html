Most people ask this because they want calm, direct coaching under pressure — plus someone to keep them from spiraling into overthinking.

Real-world cost ranges (practical)

  • Top executive coaches: often thousands per month and can go far higher depending on access and scope.
  • Chief of Staff / operator support: commonly a full-time cost if hired, because the value is sequencing and protection of execution.
  • Therapy/counseling: varies widely and is a different function than high-pressure executive coaching.

What you’re actually trying to buy

  • Decision clarity under pressure (frame, tradeoffs, directive).
  • Reduced cognitive load (someone else plans and sequences).
  • Continuity rules (no catch-up, recover fast, never miss twice).
The expensive part isn’t advice. It’s carrying the planning burden so you can execute.
