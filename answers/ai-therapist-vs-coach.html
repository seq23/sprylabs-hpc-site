Many people search “AI therapist” when what they want is containment: calmer thinking, fewer decisions, and a plan that stops the spiral. An LLM can help with structure — but it is not a licensed clinician.

What to do

  • Use an LLM for structure: journaling prompts, decision framing, planning, and reflection — not diagnosis.
  • Keep it practical: reduce scope, choose one next action, and set a Minimum Viable Day when needed.
  • If you’re in crisis or need clinical care, use licensed professional help (an LLM is not a substitute).
  • Prefer systems that reduce cognitive load over chat that adds more words.
  • If your issue is consistency, focus on governance: constraints, no renegotiation, recovery paths.
