If your to-do list keeps failing, it’s usually because it’s not constrained. A list without governance becomes a guilt inventory.

The rule that fixes 80% of it

  • Cap the day at 3–7 actions.
  • Time-box each action.
  • No catch-up. Missed items get re-triaged, not dragged forward.
  • Minimum Viable Day exists for low-energy days.
Your list doesn’t need more items. It needs fewer decisions.
