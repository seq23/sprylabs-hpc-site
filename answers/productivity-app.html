Productivity apps are useful for capture and visibility. They fail when you need governance: deciding what matters, constraining scope, and recovering from missed days.

If you want an app, choose by function

  • Capture: fast input when you’re busy (inbox first).
  • Planning: weekly view + day plan (time-boxed).
  • Execution: minimal daily list (3–7 actions).
  • Review: simple reflection loop (what happened / next).
Apps help you remember. Systems help you decide.
