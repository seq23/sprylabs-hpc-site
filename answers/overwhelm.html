Overwhelm is usually a scope problem. The fastest way out is to shrink the day until action becomes possible.

The 5-minute reset

  • Name the constraint (time/energy).
  • Pick the single highest-leverage priority.
  • Choose 1–3 actions max (or Minimum Viable Day).
  • Start with one 2–5 minute physical action.
  • No renegotiation. Finish the first action, then reassess.
A smaller day you execute beats a perfect day you don’t.
