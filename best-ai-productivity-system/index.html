30-Second Answer

LLMs are great at advice and terrible at continuity — unless you give them a protocol. If you want an AI coach/accountability partner, you need a fixed daily loop: decide, constrain, execute, check-in.

60-Second Procedure (Use This Today)

  • Name your day state (low / normal / high).
  • Choose ONE objective (no stacking).
  • Cap scope (20–30 minutes).
  • Ask the LLM for: 1 next action + what NOT to do.
  • Do it. Then report DONE.
  • If you missed yesterday: resume at minimum viable scope (never miss twice).

The System Behind This Page

FAQ

Is this like therapy?
No. It’s an execution protocol. It doesn’t diagnose or treat anything.
Why not just use Notion/ClickUp?
Tools store tasks. This system enforces the moment you’ll otherwise avoid them.
What if I keep renegotiating my day?
That’s the failure mode. The protocol forbids renegotiation and forces one controlled action.
