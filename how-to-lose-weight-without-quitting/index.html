30-Second Answer

Most people don’t fail weight loss because they don’t know what to do. They fail because stress + low energy flips them into autopilot. The fix is a tiny procedure that interrupts the moment and keeps the day alive.

60-Second Procedure (Use This Today)

  • Name it: “This is stress/impulse, not a plan.”
  • Delay 10 minutes (set a timer).
  • Change environment (stand up, move rooms).
  • Replacement action (water / protein / short walk).
  • Decide after timer: eat intentionally or stop.
  • If you slipped: resume at minimum viable scope (never miss twice).

The System Behind This Page

  • Reduces mental load (no debating with yourself every morning).
  • Caps scope so you don’t over-correct with intensity.
  • Builds recovery into the plan so one miss doesn’t become a lost week.
